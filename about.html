About Us

Delicious food since 2007

Since 2007 MIXED has been creating refining dishes that mix Lebanese cuisine with Asian food culture. With this combination became the iconic twist of flavors you all know and love.

Our biggest accomplishment was providing a service that made people feel happy and to give them something new they have never had before. A loving feeling as if you were sitting at home and eating.

A Little Bit Of History

Kelly Chang - Owner

Coming from a middle class family home cooking always played an integral part in my younger days as a teenager. Coming home from a hard day of working hard at school and smelling moms amazing cooking always made me feel better no matter how hard and stressful the day has been. I come from a Chinese/Lebanese background so the food culture was very different, but together it created a fusion of flavors that give me shivers to this day. After finishing University I have decided to open MIXED to show how amazing two culinary differences can be infused into one amazing dish. ADA is here to show its customers that you can have fun and feel at home and having something brand new to taste. Enjoy the food, indulge every bite, taste the spectacular bomb of flavors ADA provides .
